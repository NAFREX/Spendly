Your privacy matters

Privacy Policy

Spendly is built on a simple promise — your financial data belongs to you, not us. Here's exactly what we collect, why, and how we keep it safe.

Last updated: June 2026

◈

Data We Collect

We collect only what's needed to run the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense records — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter yourself.
  • Usage data — basic logs like login timestamps, kept for security purposes only.

We do not collect payment details, bank credentials, or any data from your financial accounts.

◎

How We Use Your Data

Your data powers your experience — nothing else. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Display your expense history and summaries.
  • Generate category breakdowns and spending insights.
  • Keep your account secure and functional.

We never use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or run it through any kind of profiling or analytics beyond what you see in your own dashboard.

◷

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secured servers with access controls and encryption in transit. We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

You can request full deletion of your account and all associated records at any time. We'll process that promptly — no waiting periods, no questions asked.

₹

Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load the typefaces you see on screen. This causes your browser to make a direct request to Google's servers — their privacy policy governs that interaction.

That's the only third-party service involved. We have no ad networks, tracking pixels, analytics SDKs, or data brokers embedded in Spendly.

✦

Your Rights

You're in control of your data. At any time you can:

  • Access all the data we hold about you.
  • Correct any information that's inaccurate.
  • Request complete deletion of your account and data.

Just reach out and we'll take care of it.

◉

Contact Us

Questions about this policy or how your data is handled? We're easy to reach. Get in touch through the contact details on our site and we'll respond as quickly as we can.
